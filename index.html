BetterSelf Healing & Research (BetterSelf H&R for short) is an innovative mental health start-up that brings psychotherapy/counseling to the masses. It is also home to Professional Therapists* for hire. It aims to make psychotherapy/counseling accessible to everyone at convenient cost, while making the experience as refreshing as possible. No rigid paperwork, no complicated service procedures, just a chilled atmosphere.

Mission

To be the go-to service provider of psychotherapy (and related services) for all. To make mental health services a convenient, refreshing and affordable part of everyday life.

Problem Statement

1. For a really long time, professional therapy has been a privilege only reserved for the rich and the well-off who could afford the high costs of the desperately needed service essential to our lives.

2. The majority of the people in society have no other choice but to rely on untrained religious leaders- who give no regard to scientific understanding of the world- for their psychological, emotional, behavioral and spiritual wellbeing. When religious practices fail to address their issues, people are left vulnerable and end up worse than before.

3. Mental health is only taken seriously when a person's health falls apart to the extent that he/she needs to be hospitalized at a psychiatric institution. Consequently, mental health becomes a taboo topic

Products

In order to truly bring therapy to the masses, we believe that everyone should have a backup plan for those moments when life happens and challenges become overwhelming. BetterSelf H&R offers coverage for your mental, emotional, behavioral and spiritual wellbeing. It's convenient, refreshing and ridiculously affordable! The following "insurance" packages are meant for individuals only.

  • Basic
  • Popular
  • Rare

Basic

Finally there is a backup plan for those moments when life happens and you need to talk to somebody but find that you cannot afford counselling. The Basic package is meant for anyone who understands that you may not need counselling right now but sooner or later, it will come in handy when challenges that overwhelm you arise. It is a chilled insurance plan that is for anyone who is not in a hurry. It covers costs for only two (2) sessions. By the way, when you are insured with us and you are unable to come to our offices for your counselling session, we can conduct your session online. But even if you are still stuck in that situation and need internet data to attend your session, we got you! We will assist you with M5 worth of data so that you attend your session.

Popular

The popular package is meant for the majority of people. This is the insurance package that "everyone" is choosing. It offers more hours and has a shorter waiting period. This is for people who would rather have more hours to talk to a counselor if ever life throws them challenges. So join the movement and join the ranks of society where people have a backup plan for their mental/emotional wellbeing. Unhealthy coping mechanisms are a thing of the past now, why get left behind? Oh yeah, online sessions allow you to have access to free M5 data IF YOU NEED IT, every single online session. All you have to do is to tell our reception staff to help you out when you are stuck and need to attend an online session. Now isn't that refreshing!

Rare

You know you are unique, there is no one like you and therefore you are a rare gem. The rare package is meant for you as an individual who likes the power of options. Why be limited to only normal working hours when you can have access to counselling services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week? As a unique person, you have POWER; you have the power to help others. The rare package allows you to help anyone (not just family) to recieve counselling services under your name. You get more sessions (3) and longer sessions (2 hours each), at a shorter waiting period (just 2 months). Show us anyone more rare than you, we'll wait!

Safety
We are fully aware of the online scams where thiefs steal people's money. We understand that this should be a concern for you when working with us and therefore know that your concerns are validated.

Payment methods
Since we are a young company, our methods of payment are not as refined as they should be. Please bear with us as we work on adopting more convenient payment systems.

Currently we accept payments through a service called Mokuru. It works this way;
      1. You open a Mokuru account
      2. You select the country from which you are transacting and the country to which you are transacting money
      3. you choose a registered finacial services provider from whom we will withdraw your payment. That's it!
